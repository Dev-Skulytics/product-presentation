06 / Validation

Smarter validation coverage

Before MANUAL SPOT-CHECK
  • 10–20 SKUs checked out of 1,000 total
  • ~1–2% coverage — mostly guesswork
  • No statistical basis for sample selection
  • Issues slipping through to clients undetected
Now STATISTICAL SAMPLING
  • 286 SKUs — mathematically derived minimum
  • 95% confidence level, ±5% margin of error
  • Finite population correction applied (N = 1,000)
  • Higher data accuracy with a defensible standard
COCHRAN FORMULA — FINITE POPULATION
n₀ = Z² · p · (1−p) / e²
n = n₀ / (1 + (n₀ − 1) / N)
